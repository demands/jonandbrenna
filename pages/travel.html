Things to do:

  • Woodstock, New York
    The town that gave its name to the famous 1969 music festival. There are many restaurants, boutique shops, and gift stores here.
  • Overlook Mountain
    This popular trail just outside of the town of Woodstock takes you to the top of Overlook Mountain, which gives you an excellent view of the Catskill Mountain Range and the Hudson Valley. The trail begins next to a Zen Buddhist monestary, and takes you past the ruins of the old Overlook Mountain Hotel. There is a picnic area on the summit, as well as a fire tower that offers great views.
  • Maverick Concert Hall
    Listen to classical chamber music, jazz, and world music at a rustic concert hall in the woods. The Maverick Concert Hall has been playing music every summer since 1916.
  • Storm King Art Center
    A 500-acre sculpture park in the Hudson Valley, about an hour south of Woodstock. A gorgeous landscape with more than 100 sculptures.
  • Hudson River Cruises
    Take a tour of the historic shoreline of the Hudson River on the deck of the riverboat Rip Van Winkle.
  • Whitewater Tubing on the Esopus Creek
    If the weather's hot, spend an afternoon floating down a five-mile stretch of river in the Catskill Mountains. Town Tinker Tube Rental offers equipment, tubing instructions, and safety tips. There are two routes, one for novices and one for experts.
